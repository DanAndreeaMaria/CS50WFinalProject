A gentle note about this app

This app is designed as a personal wellbeing journal. It helps you reflect on your daily mood, energy, stress and habits over time.

The insights and charts are based only on the information you choose to provide. They are meant to support self-awareness and not provide medical or psychological advice.

This app does not diagnose conditions, replace professional care or offer medical treatment or advice of any kind.

If you are feeling overwhelmed or struggling with your mental health, reaching out to a trusted person or a qualified professional may help better.

Take care of yourself and use this journal at your own pace! ❤
